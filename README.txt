Zoom Scheduler
A utility application to help you to never again be late for your classes! 
Start by opening the prereq\Zoom_loc.txt file, past the path to the zoom application, which can be found by searching fo zoom in search, and opening its file location. Copy the path to Zoom.exe, and paste it into Zoom_loc.txt
Open the Zoom Scheduler.exe file, enter upto 10 meeting id's, passwords, and times. Click done, and you are good to go! You may close the window if you like...
